ABOUT US.

At Inju KBBQ, we bring the rich, traditional flavors of Korea's charming old towns straight to your plate. Our specialty lies in crafting authentic Korean BBQ with time-honored marinades, allowing you to experience the soulful, rustic side of Korean cuisine. Every bite is a step back in time to the bustling markets and cozy streets where these flavors were perfected. We're deeply grateful that you've chosen Inju KBBQ, and we look forward to sharing this authentic journey with you time and time again.
