About Us

Welcome to our haven of sizzling delights, where every cut, every marinade, and every moment is crafted with passion. At our Korean BBQ haven, we take pride in offering an exceptional dining experience that revolves around premium meats, exquisite marinades, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.

We proudly open our doors to welcome you on a journey of flavors and hospitality. Born from a love for quality meats, exceptional marinades, and the joy of shared moments, our restaurant brings the sizzle of Korean barbecue to the cultural tapestry of Maryland.

Thank you for choosing DON as your destination for Korean BBQ in Maryland. Here's to many more sizzling moments, delightful flavors, and memories created around our table.

With gratitude and the warmest of wishes,

The DON Korean BBQ Family
